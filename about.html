Mission

TestingNation Wellness provides an accessible, self-paced platform that empowers individuals to improve their mental and emotional wellbeing through research-informed tools and guided reflection.

Vision

To become a global catalyst for sustainable mental health, where evidence-based wellness tools are a free and fundamental resource for every individual's journey toward resilience.

Core Values

Evidence-Based Integrity: We ground our resources in proven psychological and psychiatric methods, including CBT, DBT, and mindfulness, to ensure every tool is effective and safe.

Radical Accessibility: We believe wellness tools should be free, self-paced, and available on-demand, removing the barriers to personal growth.

Empowered Self-Discovery: We provide the framework for reflection and journaling, but we honor the individual as the expert of their own experience.

Complementary Care: We recognize our role as a powerful "add-on" to traditional mental health care, prioritizing a holistic approach that supports—but never replaces—professional therapy.

Important Safety Notice

TestingNation Wellness is a self-guided resource and is not a replacement for professional therapy or emergency services. If you are in immediate danger, experiencing a medical or psychiatric emergency, or in need of urgent help, please get to a safe place and call 911 (US/Canada), 112 (Europe), or your local emergency number immediately.

You can also reach the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline by calling or texting 988 (in the US) for 24/7 confidential support.

Safety & Crisis Awareness: We prioritize user safety above all else. While our platform supports daily wellbeing, we explicitly direct users to professional emergency services—such as 911 or 112—when immediate clinical or physical intervention is required.
